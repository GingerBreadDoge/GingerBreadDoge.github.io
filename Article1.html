13 colonies broke away from England!

Friday July 15, 1776

Jacob K

Topic: Republic,
The 13 colonies just broke away from England, so they all came together to create a republic, but a republic is a former form of government where the power is in the people's hands to vote for their leaders and make laws. This is often done through voting in elections. The people elected representatives to make laws and manage the country on their behalf. Also, the people can vote to get the person out of office if they do not do a good job. A republic typically has a constitution that outlines the rights and responsibilities of the government and the people. The point of a republic is to have a fair system that applies equally to all citizens, regardless of their background or status, which means all citizens in a republic have the freedom to live their lives as they see it.
